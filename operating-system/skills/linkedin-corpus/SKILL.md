# LinkedIn Corpus

Convert LinkedIn examples into reusable content intelligence.

## Workflow
1. Load the canonical niche map when available and select the exact lane.
2. Extract observable mechanics only: source, creator, format, hook mechanic, proof mechanism, emotional driver, CTA type, why it worked, and JT translation.
3. Save high-quality examples to the swipe/corpus system.
4. Update creator targets and pattern library.
5. Reject generic motivation, guru tone, vague AI hype, or examples that cannot translate into JT's practical operator voice.

## Quality Bar
- Prioritize consulting/proof/operator lanes.
- X examples do not substitute for LinkedIn examples.
- Product/app examples are allowed only when they support a current app marketing objective.

## Output
Return accepted examples, rejected examples with reason, files updated, and remaining corpus gaps.
