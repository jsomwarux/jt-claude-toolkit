# Proposal PDF

Use for client-facing proposals and priced scopes. Drafts are internal until JT reviews and sends.

## House Style
- Split header: title/subtitle left, prepared-for/date right.
- 4px blue accent line: `#4A6CF7` to `#6B8AFF`.
- Uppercase section labels in blue.
- Use-case cards: title left, price right, recommendation note in blue italic.
- Investment table: all line items reconcile to total.
- Footer: `JT Somwaru | jtsomwaru.com`.

## Workflow
1. Collect client/contact, title/subtitle, use cases, prices, timeline, support, assumptions, and access needed.
2. Fill the proposal HTML template or create matching HTML.
3. Render to PDF with WeasyPrint when available.
4. Verify no placeholders, no secrets, pricing reconciliation, and proof-safe language.

## Content Rules
Use independent AI implementation consultant framing. Price outcomes, not tasks. Public client claims require permission.
